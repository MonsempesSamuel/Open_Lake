Personal data

Account management and trackers
OpenLake follows the recommendations of the CNIL in terms of data collection, tracers and cookies. In accordance with the amended law n ° 78-17 of 6 January 1978 (known as the “Informatique et Liberté” law).
To have an account, you only need to provide us with a valid email address, username and password. All other information is optional.
The site does not include any statistical survey tool. The IPs are thus anonymized and the data is collected by a specific server that we host.
For any other question concerning questions relating to privacy or to express your opposition to data processing, you can contact our data protection officer: dpo@open-lake.fr.
Secure distribution
OpenLake offers its visitors access to its site and to secure connection devices (HTTPS).
